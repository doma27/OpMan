Mix

Pressing the Filter Cycle Mix button push-button allows you to introduce graphite additive directly into the tank. The mixer is turned on and starts an initial mix to distribute the additive.

When the additive has been added you will have to press a further push-buttons that are located at the top of the steps above the tanks (pictured below) to confirm that the additive has been added before the system moves to the next stage in the process.
 

Press the T1 or T2 button depending on which tank you have just filled with additive.
