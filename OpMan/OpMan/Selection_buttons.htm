Radio buttons

This kind of selection button is the most common. It allows the selection of one and only one item from a particular group.

The icons for these buttons are displayed on a grey background when they are not selected. The background changes to a chocolate-brown background when the button is selected.

View selection buttons

You will typically find these buttons on a green panel at the bottom of the screen.


 All these background colours are configurable, this is the default colour configuration only.

Often when you click these selection buttons, you will find that the view in the main part of the screen changes to a different view to mirror the context of the part or sub-system of the machine that you have selected.

Checkboxes

These operate as another way of making selections, for example in the Power settings test area of the machine (see Maintenance manual for details), each checkbox  shown in the red rectangle  below represents an individual electrode.

The background for checkbox buttons are coloured grey when they are not selected and change to dark blue when they are selected.
