Roughness parameters as defined in ISO 4287

Some parameters of roughness that you will encounter are:

Ra - Roughness average 

The standard parameter of roughness that surface texture is measured in.

PC - Peak Count

The number of local peaks which project through a selectable band centred about the mean line or a line parallel to it. The count is determined only over the evaluation length though the results are given in peaks per cm (or per inch)

Rsk - Skewness

Rsk is a measure of the symmetry of the profile about the mean line.

This parameter indicates whether the spikes on the surface are predominately negative or positive or if the profile has an even distribution of peaks and valleys.

For further parameters refer to the Appendix in the Maintenance Manual headed ‘Optimisation of Texturing Parameters’.
