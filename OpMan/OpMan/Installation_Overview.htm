Installation Overview

The following overview diagram shows you the main component areas of a typical site installation.

 

Roll manipulator

§  Contains the Headstock and Tailstock

§  Provides traverse of roll or texture station

Dielectric Plant

§  Stores, circulates and filters dielectric oil via a system of feed tanks, trenches and pumps.

Control room

§  Allows you to control your EDT machines remotely from a separate location.

Main control panel and cabinets

§  Provides power to the User control panel and servo controls

Fume extraction / fire protection unit

§  The Fume Extraction System is a freestanding unit that house three filter stages, these filters remove oil, mist and dust particles.
