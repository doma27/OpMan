Electrical Cabinets

The machine is fed with mains power via three or four Bay Drives and Power Cabinets. From here power is fed to the other machine components. A drive cabinet delivers power to the Spindle and the Saddle.

 

A power cabinet delivers power to the electrodes. The machines will have the same number of power cabinets as drop arms.

For example a 72-electrode machine would have six power cabinets each with 12 power cards.

The Servos on a particular station are controlled by the electronics in a Servo cabinet.
