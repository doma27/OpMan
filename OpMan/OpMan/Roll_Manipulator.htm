Roll Manipulator

The roll manipulator consists of a machined cast iron bed and two Steadies - the parts that support the roll.

 

These steadies support the roll with its bearing journals on white metal Steady Pads during the texturing operation.

The Steady pads are constantly lubricated to prevent overheating of the bearing surface; this also reduces wear on the pads.

To ensure that the roll stays within the working limits of the bed, there are limit switches positioned at either end. These are monitored by the computer control system on the Operator Console.

On activation, these switches will stop movement of the machine components and display an error on the Console.
