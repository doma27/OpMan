Positions and Negative & Positive directions of travel

Each machine has a physical rule running the whole length of the machine that measures distances.

 

The measurements start with low values at the Headstock end and end at high values near the Tailstock end.

As the various subsystems of the machine move towards the Tailstock end of the machine, they are referred to as travelling in a Positive (+ive) direction. As the component parts travel backwards again towards the Headstock end, they are referred to as heading in a Negative (-ive) direction.
