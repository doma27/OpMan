Turning the EDT Machine On

Before you turn the machine on, you need to ensure that you have set the Isolator switch to the ‘On’ position.

You will find this situated at the end of the Drives and Distribution cabinets.

NOTE: The isolator switch has a hole suitable for a padlock through it to allow you to secure the machine when it is not in use.

At this stage, you will only see the Input lights on the front doors of the power cabinets lit up.

You will need to wait around 45 seconds before you can turn the EDT machine on at the User Console; to turn the machine on:

§  Press the green button next to the Emergency stop button.

You will hear air in the valves as the machine starts up. On the user interface of the console you will see a series of icons representing different areas of the installation appear.

While these icons are displayed, the machine is doing internal checks of all the areas of the site required for the machine to operate. This is known as the Power up Sequence.

If these icons do not have a green background, the power up sequence will fail. Feedback text relating to the area that has failed the internal test will be displayed in the area below these icons.

You will also see the red push-button  below the Console screen appear lit up.

 

After the Power-up sequence has finished, you will see the Home screen on the console User interface. The output lights on the front of the cabinets will also be lit up.

NOTE: In the top right-hand area of the Home screen you will see the version of the software that you are running.
