Handling Tank Changeover

If the filtering is at the READY stage and the ‘ready tank’ is above the working level, then when feeding is complete, check the dielectric life.

NOTE: If the dielectric life exceeds the MSD pre-set limit, consider switching tanks.

When the tanks are switched (i.e. Tank 1 online to Tank 2 online) the return valves must be switched over and the relevant mixers switched on or off. The mixer for the on-line tank is always on; so, for example, if Tank 2 is ready and Tank 1 is on-line and they are then switched, then the mixer on Tank 2 must be turned on and the mixer on Tank 1 must be turned off.
