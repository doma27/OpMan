Manual and AUTO modes

The following sections describe the processes that occur for the filtering process. Each stage of the process can be operated in AUTOmatic or Manual mode.

If an error occurs while running in AUTO mode, use the schematic diagram on the mimic panel to diagnose the valve or pump where the error has occurred. To clear the filter faults after you have fixed the cause of the reported error, press the red  push-button on the Dielectric Mimic Panel.

NOTE: You can also consult the operator console where you will find these types of faults reported as Warnings in blue on the Fault and Warnings log. See Fault Reporting and Help for further details.

In AUTO, the panel will return to AUTO mode, wait and then flash all the cycle and start buttons

The buttons on the Console use colour coding that is similar to the schematic on the mimic panel:

Green represents the carbon graphite additive being added to the dielectric oil. Red represents cake and discharge in ‘dirty’ used dielectric and Yellow represents the flow of clean dielectric.
