Filter

The process starts to filter the used dielectric from the feed tank into the clean tank. The mixer is activated (if the dielectric fluid is above the ‘Mix’ level and begins dispersing the solids in the dielectric.

Dielectric is pumped through a pipeline to the bottomof the filter column.

The dielectric passes through a series of filter sleeves that are wrapped around cylindrical mesh filter elements. These remove solid particles from the dielectric.

The clean, filtered dielectric fills thetop section of the filter column and eventually flows into the Clean Dielectric Tank 3. Because the solids content is high, the filters gradually cake up.
