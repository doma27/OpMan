E stop

The majority of E-stops are keyless. These can be both activated and de‑activated by the user.

 

 

 

Keyless E-stops

 

E-stops with keys

 

To de‑activate an E-stop, twist and rotate the red button so that it springs out again towards you.

When you press an E-stop button, all electrical power that is being distributed to motors, indicators and pumps is shut down immediately.

NOTE: On the power cabinets you will see the 24V input lights remaining on; the 24V output lights will go off (for details of where to locate these lights, see the section on Turning On and Off).

After you have activated an E-stop you will also see power-up sequence icons with a red background similar to those shown below and an explanation of the reason for failing to turn on.
