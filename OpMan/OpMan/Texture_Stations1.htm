Texture Stations 

§  Check bath seals for signs of excessive wear or damage and replace if necessary.

§  Check bath for debris build-up and clean where necessary.

§  Check electrodes and sleeves for signs of excessive wear or damage and replace where necessary.

§  If there is a build-up of graphite on the electrodes then clean the electrodes and sleeves with clean dielectric using a clean stiff 45mm paintbrush

§  Visually inspect the dielectric supply to the bath for signs of leakage, crimping, trapping or chaffing.
