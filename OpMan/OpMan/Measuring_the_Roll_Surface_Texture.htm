Measuring the Roll Surface Texture

The aim of measuring the roll surface texture is to achieve a specific measure of roughness over the production life of the roll.

To measure the surface texture, use a portable measuring device such as those found in the Taylor Hobson Surtronic range of surface texture monitoring kits pictured below:

This allows you to take mechanical measurements of the roll using the stylus and calibration standard reference specimen contained in the kit.

For further details, refer to a handbook downloadable from the Taylor Hobson website.
