Statistical surveys

As part of controlling and measuring the surface texture, you are trying to get an average figure that equates to your desired target for surface roughness.

This will be achieved by a systematic survey process of measuring, texturing and recording over the production life of several rolls using the same program on the same type of roll.

If you then plot these measurements, this will then allow you to make micro-adjustments to the texturing program and to achieve your precise desired target measurement of roughness.

NOTE: Measurements of this kind can be affected by temperature and weather conditions so we recommend that you attempt to take measurements in environmental conditions that are as similar as possible and that you check the calibration of your surface measuring kit regularly.
