The Mimic Panel gives an overview diagram of the routes taken by the dielectric through a system of valves and pumps between the tanks from the point where it is fed clean into the system to the point where it is filtered for reuse and finally discharged.

Filtering errors are indicated both on the user interface of the Operator console, and on the Dielectric Plant Mimic Panel.
