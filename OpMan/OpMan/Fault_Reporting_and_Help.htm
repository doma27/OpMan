Fault Reporting and Help

 
 

You can view a full log of status messages relating to the installation for example where the machine has an error on power-up by pressing the Exclamation mark icon  in the bottom right-hand corner of the screen.

To clear all messages, click the dustbin icon .

For a detailed description of an error and the text relating to it, press the line of text relating to the error or click the  icon in the bottom right-hand corner.

In the screen that appears, you will find a more detailed Description relating to the errors on the machine and where appropriate a Document Reference that relates to the engineering drawing of the machine.

You can limit what messages are shown to you in the status log by day, week, month, or since the start of messages being logged on the machine using the icons in the top right-hand corner of the screen.
