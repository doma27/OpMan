Other User interface conventions

Left-hand and right-hand pictures are applied in the corner of some icons so that you can instantly see whether you are looking at an area that is on the left- or right-hand side of the EDT machine.

 

 

 

Left-hand side icon

Indicates that the Servo and Electrode are located on the Left-hand side of machine

 

 

Right-hand side icon

Indicates that the Servo and Electrode are located on the right-hand side of the machine
