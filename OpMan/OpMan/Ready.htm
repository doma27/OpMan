Ready

The mixture is ready and kept in a mix cycle holding period where the mixture is repeatedly mixed and distributed for a short time at intermittent periods – normally half an hour apart.

While the mixing occurs the system monitors how long the on-line tank has been working and after a pre-set time automatically switches over the on-line and filtering tanks so that the recently filtered dielectric can start being used. Meanwhile the filtering process can start again on the tank which has just come ‘off-line’.
