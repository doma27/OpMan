Typical roll survey

When you survey a roll you take readings at regular intervals along the roll to ensure the texturing is consistent and repeatable.

To take measurements of the surface texture along a roll, you need to take measurements at various even distances (labelled A, B and C in the diagram below) along the face of a roll and at 90 degree intervals around the circumference of the roll (labelled 1 -4 in the diagram below).

You can then plot the roughness at these measuring points. This will give you a total of twelve measurement results that you can then plot and use in a similar fashion to the spread sheet below.

(STD_DEV / MEAN) * 100.0
