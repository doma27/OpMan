Dielectric Fluid Plant

This component is the area of the plant that filters, mixes and provides the dielectric used to prevent any air reaching the sparks that are being used in the texturing process.

The component is designed and manufactured to function as a standalone unit with all the necessary electrical controls and distributed I/O station built in.

The controls on the system can be configured so that the filtration process can be carried out automatically or manually via illuminated push button controls.
